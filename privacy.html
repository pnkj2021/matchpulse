Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Information stored on your device

MatchPulse uses browser local storage to remember followed teams, display preferences, alert settings and recent score snapshots. This information stays in your browser and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Notifications

Match alerts require browser permission. MatchPulse only attempts to display an alert after you enable the control and your browser grants permission.

External services

The website requests cricket data from SportScore and font files from Google Fonts. Those providers may receive standard technical information such as your IP address and browser headers under their respective privacy policies.

Analytics and accounts

MatchPulse currently has no user accounts and does not include an analytics or advertising tracker. This policy will be updated before either is introduced.

Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the project channel listed on the contact page.
