About MatchPulse

Cricket scores with the context intact.

MatchPulse is an independent cricket prototype designed around a simple idea: a score should help people understand a match, not force them to decode it. The match desk combines current score data with concise summaries, personal score history and clearly labelled analytical context.

Editorial principles

Trust comes before spectacle. MatchPulse does not invent momentum, probabilities or turning points when a data feed cannot support them. Demonstration stories are labelled as demonstration content, while current scores show their provider and update state.

Data and independence

Current cricket scores are supplied by SportScore. MatchPulse is not affiliated with a cricket board, team, competition or betting operator. The service is informational and does not provide wagering advice.
